Reflection

Personal Reflection

So far in my journey into the technology field, I have learned a lot and I have many things to reflect on already. I have been a part of several different projects at this point in time and each one has taught me something new. I have always been a visual and kinesthetic learner and accomplishing these projects allowed me to truly ingrain the multiple things I had learned. At this point I have mainly participated in digital media projects and I honestly have really enjoyed these types of projects.

Looking back I have always been interested in different digital media projects from video editing, commercials that use digital effects, photo editing, and so much more. I had always wanted to learn how different video effects were created and applied and because of this I have been invested in learning all that I can. I was taught in various classes different techniques on how to apply different effects in Adobe After Effects. I am proud of how much I have learned and how well I am able to apply the techniques I have learned. I now have the capability to produce work that is professional, realistic, and attention grabbing.

I have also discovered that I have a passion for and enjoy the creation of websites. So far I have done more and know more of the front end side but I have been interested and plan on learning more on the backend side of web development. I have learned a bit of Javascript and I have learned a bit about how servers play into web development. I have also always been interested in AR and VR and when I first heard of photogrammetry I was instantly curious. I have since learned how to make models that can be accessed in VR or AR using different methods of photogrammetry.

Carrer Goals

When it comes to my career, my main goal is that I want to obtain a job in a digital media setting. Since I have always been interested in digital media I know that I will be invested in the work that I do. With being invested I hope to expand my skill set and learn as much as I can. My goal is to constantly produce work that I am proud of. I want to produce work that would make my younger self, who had no idea where to even begin learning how to create these kinds of things, proud of how far I have come and learned. I would like to be able to be an example of how anyone can learn how to create the things that they enjoy.
